Game Guide

Objectives

  • Reinforce Module 9 competencies through interactive play.
  • Provide immediate feedback and adaptive difficulty.

Materials

  • Device with a modern browser.
  • Optional projector for whole-class play.

Mechanics

  • Answer multiple-choice questions before the timer ends.
  • Earn points for correct answers; lose a life for wrong ones.
  • Complete all questions or until lives run out.

Scoring

  • +10 points per correct answer.
  • Time bonus added at the end of each question.

Assessment

  • Use the final score and accuracy to gauge mastery.
  • Review missed questions after the game.
